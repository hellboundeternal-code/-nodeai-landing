Privacy Policy

How Node.AI collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

👋 Overview

Node.AI ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Ryan Berry. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Node.AI mobile application and website (getnode.dev).

By using Node.AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Authentication data — OAuth tokens from providers you connect (Google, GitHub, etc.)
  • Payment information — subscription and billing data processed securely by Stripe
  • App usage data — how you interact with agents, features, and connectors
  • Device identifiers — device ID for push notification delivery
  • Crash and diagnostic data — error logs to improve app stability

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Node.AI service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send important service notifications and updates
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To personalize your agent and connector experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

🔗 Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Clerk — authentication and user management
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Fly.io — backend infrastructure hosting
  • Expo / EAS — mobile app delivery and push notifications

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

🔐 Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time:

  • Visit getnode.dev/delete-account
  • Email us at hellboundeternal@gmail.com with subject "Delete My Account — Node.AI"

Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax law. All other personal data is deleted within 30 days of your request.

👶 Children's Privacy

Node.AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

🌍 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hellboundeternal@gmail.com.
