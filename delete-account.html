Delete Your
Node.AI Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and all associated data.

🗑️

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send an email to our support team
Email [email protected] with the subject line: "Delete My Account — Node.AI"
2
Include your account email address
Provide the email address associated with your Node.AI account so we can locate and verify your account.
3
Confirmation within 5 business days
We will confirm receipt of your request and complete the deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a final confirmation email when deletion is complete.
🗑️ Request Account Deletion ← Back to Node.AI
📋

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action
Account profile (name, email) Deleted immediately
AI agents & configurations Deleted immediately
Chat history & conversations Deleted immediately
Connected app credentials Deleted immediately
Subscription & payment info Retained 90 days (legal/tax)
Transaction records & receipts Retained 7 years (tax compliance)
Anonymous usage analytics Retained (no personal identifiers)
⚠️ Payment and billing records may be retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations. These records cannot be linked back to your account after deletion.
💳

Active Subscriptions

If you have an active Node.AI subscription (Starter, Pro, or Business), please cancel it before requesting account deletion to avoid further charges.

1
Open Node.AI → go to Settings → Subscription
2
Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm
3
Then submit your account deletion request using the button above
